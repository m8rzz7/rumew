what us RU Mew!

Excellent Question...

RU Mew is a collaborative project that combines the talents of a team of passionate and dedicated individuals, each with their own unique skills and expertise. The project is focused on developing engaging and immersive content that provides insight into the world of Rutgers University-New Brunswick, as told from my perspective as the author and creator, Mew.

At the heart of RU Mew is the belief in the power of storytelling. Our goal is to construct narratives through the Mew Reviews that encapsulate the essence of being here at the largest college in New Jersey. Whether it's through the vivid images captured by our talented photographer Oshawott, the aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly site brought to life by our web designer Jirachi, or the eloquently articulated content refined by our meticulous editor Snorlax, every element of our work is thoughtfully crafted to tell a story.
