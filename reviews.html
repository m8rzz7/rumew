more RU

Rutgers Rarities

Are you interested in all things supernatural at Rutgers University? Rutgers Rarities has got you covered! This site showcases over 45 stories investigating hauntings, weird animals, and old abandoned buildings on campus.

The Medium

The Medium is a satirical news outlet created by Rutgers students, similar to The Onion These articles are usually comedically critical of the academic institution which the writers attend, and some are entirely unrelated and focus on pop-culture.

The Daily Targum

If you're looking for real news and insight on Rutgers written by students, look no further than the Daily Targum! The Daily Targum is actually the second-oldest college new organization (founded in 1869) and one of the largest. The website has a wide variety of article topics, ranging from news, sports, opinion pieces, and more! There is a photo gallery section displaying the vastly diverse community at Rutgers.

Her Campus--Rutgers

Her Campus is a media platform devoted to empowering college women globally. It was founded by and for college women and centers on the unique experience of navigating this transformative stage of life. According to their Wikipedia page, As of 2024, Her Campus Chapter Network consists of more than 380 chapters worldwide, with more than 7,500 chapter members, including Rutgers University! Additionally, the site has even featured articles relating to Pokémon as well (read Top 5 Pokémon™ GO Hot Spots on College Avenue).

Complete Pokémon™ Pokédex

For those interested in learning more about any Pokémon from the 9 generations, check out this database, which is complete with each of their main stats. The database is by default listed in numeric order from 0001 to 1025, and be switched to reverse order. There's also an option to sort by type.

Bulbagarden--Mew Wiki

Do you want to know more about Mew? This page covers a very in-depth, comprehensive biography on Mew. The site also has detailed information on other Pokémon, with a list of Pokémon by Nation Pokédex number.

Pokémon™ Showdown

Have you ever wanted to compete in a Pokémon battle? Pokémon Showdown can make your dreams come true! This site virtual simulates battles which can be played in browser or as a desktop application.
